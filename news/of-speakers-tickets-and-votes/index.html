2019-06-19

Of speakers, tickets, and votes

Talks and workshops are up, early bird tickets are nigh, and the community vote is about to begin.

Talks and workshops announced

You blew us away with 264 applications this year. The talk selection committee powered through all of them and chose the lineup for this year.

We're still working out details with some speakers, but most of the talks are published. Invitations for the community vote and rejections will go out soon.

View Talks View Workshops

End of early bird tickets

The last day to get tickets at the amazing price of 300kn for individuals and 400kn for companies is this Sunday, 2019-06-23. Regular tickets cost twice as much, so don’t miss out.

As an added benefit early bird ticket holders get to choose the final talks in the community vote.

Buy tickets

Community vote

As has become customary, all early bird ticket holders get a chance to influence the conference lineup by voting for talk submissions which were highly rated, but didn’t quite make the cut.

If you have an early bird ticket, you will receive an email invite to the community vote soon. You may vote for as many or as few talks as you wish.

The vote starts on Monday 2019-06-24 and closes on Sunday, 2019-06-30 and two top rated talks will join our speaker lineup.
