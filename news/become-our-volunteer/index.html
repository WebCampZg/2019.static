Become a Volunteer

Be a part of the WebCamp Zagreb 2019 conference by applying to volunteer.

WebCamp Zagreb is a community-driven conference oriented toward technology that powers todayâ€™s web. It is a four-day event in Zagreb that will bring together 40+ speakers and 800+ attendees, sharing their expertise, learning, networking and job hunting.

One of the ways to get involved is by becoming a volunteer. Volunteering is fun and allows you to meet new people - speakers, organizers, other volunteers, and of course visitors.

If you wish to be a member of the crew making sure things go smoothly, you're welcome to join us. Please, fill in the application form and we'll get in touch soon.
